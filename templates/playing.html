What is Playing with Programming?

Playing with Programming is the result of a group project required during the Software Development course known as the GAPT. This project is meant for students to apply what they have learnt throughout the course to create an industry standard solution.

Each group of students are given a different project. Our group's project was to create a video game for young children aged 7-10 that teaches them the basics of programming using Lego style blocks.

I was tasked with creating the design and logic of the Lego style blocks. I also took the role of team leader and was in charge of taking the supervisor's vision and turning that vision into a solution.
