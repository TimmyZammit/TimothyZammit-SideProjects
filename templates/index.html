About Me

My name is Timothy Zammit and I have finished my final year in Software Development from the University of Malta. I am adept in a variety of technologies, however, through my course projects I developed a keen interest in frontend development as well as video game design and data structures and algorithms.

Apart from this, I also gained experience in team management and realised my eagerness to develop my talents on this area. I am highly adaptable and empathetic with a love of solving problems with relentless deteremination.

Previous Experience

GAPT - Playing With Programming

GAPT is a group assignment given in the second year of the Software Development Course and is used to see how students apply their knowledge to a real world project.
